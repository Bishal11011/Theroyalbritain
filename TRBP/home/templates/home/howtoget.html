How To Get

Easy to get there, we have various options. Can take the jeep from Kathamndu to Chheskam, then need some 2 and half days trek. Also can take flight from Kathmandu to Phaplu, then jeep ride to Chheskam. If you are physically fit, you can take a flight from Kathmandu to Lukla then walk down to Chheskam which takes some 3 full days, quite ups and downs.
